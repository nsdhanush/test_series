DEVELOPER STUDENT CLUBS

THE OXFORD COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING


Students bridge the gap between theory and practice

Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

Reasons to take up the DSC entry-test

    1. You will know the capability of your thinking capacity, logical reasoning skills and innovative skills.
    2. You will have an idea on how your placement interviews will take place by attending the test.
    3. We have designed the test according to the Google DSC guidelines, hence you will get an idea about the standards of the club as well.
    4. DSC will be able to choose the best-skilled people for the club in various aspects.
    5. Once you get selected after the tests, you will get the intra/inter college recognition which will help you in expanding your contacts.
    6. The contacts that you build will help you with your internships, placements and recruitment.
    7. Enrollment to the club will let you learn a lot of skills to build your career!
    What are you waiting for, take up the test and enroll yourself to one of the biggest Student Clubs of the world! All the best!
